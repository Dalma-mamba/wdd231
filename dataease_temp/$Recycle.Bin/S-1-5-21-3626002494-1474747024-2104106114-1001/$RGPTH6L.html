Explore Eswatini

Site Name

Explore Eswatini

This name represents a tourism website focused on showcasing the beauty, culture, and attractions of Eswatini. It is simple, clear, and easy for users to remember.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about tourist destinations in Eswatini. It will help users discover places to visit, learn about activities, and plan their trips more effectively.

Scenarios

  • What are the best tourist attractions to visit in Eswatini?
  • Where can I find information about travel and accommodation in Eswatini?

Color Schema

  • #1e293b – Used for header and footer background
  • #f5f7fa – Used for page background
  • #3b82f6 – Used for accents, links, and highlights

Typography

  • Roboto – Used for body text
